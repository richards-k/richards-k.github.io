Teletherapy for the treatment of concerns that arise in the space where mental health and physical health meet.

Dr. Spencer M. Richards, PhD

Psychologist

I work as a full-time clinical health psychologist and specialize in the treatment of concerns that arise in the space where our mental and physical health meet. Specific areas where I hold specialty training include insomnia, chronic pain, weight management and emotional eating, chronic illness management (e.g., diabetes, MS, or other significant and life limiting illnesses), and improving overall well-being.

I view physical and mental health as two sides of the same coin – you’re one whole person. I view myself as working somewhere between a therapist and a health coach, helping my clients identify what feels most important in life, what’s in the way of living how they would like to be living, and getting moving toward the best lives they can live.

I practice by the principle that the point of therapy is not needing it. To that end, I am goal-focused and use the best possible treatments available to get moving toward a better life.
